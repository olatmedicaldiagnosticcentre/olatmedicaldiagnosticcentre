Laboratory Testing Services at Olat Medical Diagnostic Centre

At Olat Medical Diagnostic Centre, we offer a comprehensive range of laboratory testing services to ensure accurate diagnosis and treatment. Our advanced facility and skilled professionals provide precise and reliable results across various departments:

Laboratory Departments:

Haematology and Serology Department:

Focused on blood disorders and serological tests for diagnosing infections and diseases. Services include:

  • Full Blood Count
  • Haemoglobin
  • Packed Cell Volume
  • White Cell Count
  • Red Cell Count
  • Differential White Cell Count
  • Platelet Count
  • Bleeding Time
  • Clotting Time
  • Prothrombin Time
  • Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate
  • Malaria Parasite
  • Widal Test
  • Blood Group and Genotype
  • Human Immunodeficiency Virus Screening
  • Hepatitis Screening
  • Heaf's Mantoux Test
  • Blood for Microfilaria
  • Parasitology Test
  • Serum Tuberculin Test

Clinical Chemistry Department:

Specializing in blood and urine analyses, including:

  • Fasting Blood Sugar
  • Random Blood Sugar
  • Two Hours Postprandial Blood Sugar
  • Electrolyte Concentration
  • Liver Function Tests
  • Total Blood Cholesterol
  • Total Blood Protein
  • Uric Acid
  • Serum Amylase
  • Full Urinalysis
  • Blood Calcium
  • Oral Glucose Tolerance Test

Hormonal Profiles and Special Tests:

Comprehensive evaluations of hormonal health and other specialized tests, including:

  • Thyroid Screening
  • Infertility Profile
  • Ovulation Profile
  • Male Infertility Profile
  • Menstrual Disorder Profile
  • Testosterone Only
  • Progesterone Only
  • Oestrogen Only
  • Prolactin Profile
  • Early Menopause Syndrome
  • Routine Medical Examination
  • Food Handlers Test
  • Prostate-Specific Antigen

Microbiology Department:

Conducting tests for the detection and identification of microorganisms, including:

  • Urine Microbiology Culture and Sensitivity and Analysis
  • High Vaginal Swab
  • Stool Microbiology Culture and Sensitivity and Analysis
  • Stool Occult Blood
  • Blood Culture
  • Sputum Microbiology Culture and Sensitivity
  • Semen Microbiology Culture and Sensitivity and Analysis
  • Urethral Swab
  • Acid-Fast Bacilli (x1, x2, x3)
  • Wound Swab
  • Throat Swab
  • Ear Swab
  • Other Swabs

At Olat Medical Diagnostic Centre, we are committed to providing accurate, timely, and comprehensive diagnostic information to support patient care and treatment decisions. With our advanced technology and dedicated professionals, you can trust us for reliable results and exceptional patient care.
